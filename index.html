About Me

Hello, Dwindra is here!

I really love robots, especially humanoid! It has been my dream since I was a kid to build my own robots. This has led to my pursuit of knowledge in robotics.

I graduated from Institut Teknologi Bandung with B.Sc in Electrical Engineering in 2014. Then, I received my M.Sc in Mechano-Informatics from The University of Tokyo in 2018.

During my Masters, I worked on a full-sized humanoid robot using articial muscles. I fabricated the robot from scratch and developed the muscle control for developing an agile humanoid robot with explosive movements.

I would love to deepen my knowledge and experience in robotics and intelligent systems.
